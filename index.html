What is AncientGeeks.org?

Ancient Geeks is a community of passionate tech enthusiasts with extensive careers in the software industry and open source. We believe in the transformative power of beautifully crafted software and strive for excellence in all our endeavors. Our mission is to build high-quality software that inspires and empowers people, using innovative software development techniques such as AI-powered code and content generation.

But our vision goes beyond just software development. We aim to partner with mission-driven organizations, providing them with advisory, mentorship, software development, and evaluation services to help them achieve their goals. We believe that technology, when used effectively, can be a powerful tool for change, and we are committed to using our skills and expertise to make a positive impact.

Join us on our journey as we explore the not-so-ancient wisdom of modern technology, and work together to create a better future through software.
